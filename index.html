Your Medical Passport

One secure card with your critical history, instantly available to responders so they can act with certainty from second one.

What’s included?
  • Identity & emergency contacts
  • Conditions, medications, allergies
  • Preferred hospital & GP
  • Language & accessibility needs
Who can see it?

Only you and authorized responders via a time‑limited, consented link. Data is end‑to‑end encrypted.

How is it secured?

Encrypted at rest and in transit. Access requires consent or emergency unlock per policy.
